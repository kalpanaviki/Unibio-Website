Penaeus Monodon

Commonly known as the giant tiger prawn or black tiger shrimp, is a species of marine crustacean that holds significant economic and culinary importance. Native to the Indo-Pacific region, renowned for its large size, distinctive dark stripes on its shell, and succulent meat.

Sustainable Environment

A sustainable environment is crucial to ensure the species' long-term viability by managing water quality, preventing pollution, and minimizing negative impacts on surrounding habitats. Sustainable practices support healthy growth and reproduction of P. monodon, while also safeguarding both ecological conservation and the continued success of P. monodon cultivation.
