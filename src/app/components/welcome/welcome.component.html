About UniBio

UniBio (India) Hatcheries is an Indian subsidiary of an international leader in production and marketing of premium quality black tiger shrimp (P. monodon) from its SPF domestication & breeding program. Our seed stocks are the outcome of three decades of proven maturation & seed production technology. We are willing to extend our expertise in nursery and grow-out phases for successful crop.

Vision

To be a leading provider of high-quality, sustainable, and genetically superior Penaeus monodon (black tiger shrimp) in India, contributing to the growth and development of the aquaculture industry.

Mission

Our mission is to produce and supply genetically superior Penaeus monodon shrimp that meet the highest quality standards, while promoting sustainable and environmentally responsible aquaculture practices. We are committed to conducting research and development to enhance the genetic potential of our shrimp stock, ensuring disease resistance, growth performance, and product quality. We aim to establish strong partnerships with shrimp farmers and industry stakeholders, providing them with technical support, training, and access to our superior shrimp genetics. Through innovation, integrity, and continuous improvement, we strive to contribute to the success and profitability of our customers and the overall development of the shrimp aquaculture sector in India.

Why Chennai

UniBio (India) Hatcheries Pvt Ltd is located at Mugaiyur which is ideally 60KM drive from Chennai in ECR. This beach side spot strategically situated along the Bay of Bengal, provides us with abundant access to a diverse range of marine resources and favorable environmental conditions for shrimp aquaculture. The coastal water also offers suitable salinity levels, water temperature, and nutrient-rich conditions necessary for shrimp hatchery operations.
