About Us

UniBio (India) Hatcheries is an Indian subsidiary of an international leader in production and marketing of premium quality black tiger shrimp (P. monodon) from its SPF domestication & breeding program. Our seed stocks are the outcome of three decades of proven maturation & seed production technology. We are willing to extend our expertise in nursery and grow-out phases for successful crop.

Testimony to Madagascar

Our broodstocks are directly imported from Madagascar. Madagascar being an isolated geography, far from other shrimp-farming countries, it is disease-free. And the producers put in significant efforts to promote quality over quantity to preserve the pristine characteristics of Madagascar. And so all partners involved are fully convinced that the only sustainable, long-term approach must be based on the maintenance of environmental integrity and total exclusion of foreign shrimp species and pathogens (SPF) complying all the sanitary norms.
